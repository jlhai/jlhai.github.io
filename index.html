About Me

As a passionate girl in Science and Technology, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Engineering Computer Science, Electricity and Electronics at the age of 15. Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Science in Statistics and Data Science. Additionally, I have a strong affinity for artificial intelligence, computer science and mathematics. My educational journey has been atypical, as I entered the University at the age of 12. My goal is to further develop my knowledge and expertise to pursue specialized research programs in these fields.
